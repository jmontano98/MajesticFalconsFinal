Information Problem: Helping people figure out what classes they should take after the transition to online.

What We Do: We provide UMD INST undergrad students a way to see the GPA trends for all of their classes
and sections from the past 3 semesters, since COVID forced us all to study online in Fall 2020. If we notice a
significant shift in the GPA, the student is warned via the Trending Down label so that they're prepared to take
a class that doesn't have good results virtually (if they still choose to).
