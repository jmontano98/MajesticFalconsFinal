About

Information Problem: Helping students determine what classes they should take after the transition to online learning.

What We Do: We provide UMD INST undergraduate students a way to see the GPA trends and Pass/Fail/Withdrawal vs. Letter Grading rates for all of their classes and sections from the past 3 semesters, since the COVID-19 pandemic forced students to take courses online in Fall 2020. Students can observe the shifts in the GPA and the Pass/Fail/Withdrawal rates from past semesters before the online transition to the semesters when and after the transition occurred. This help students get an idea how the class performed after the online transition and decide if taking a class virtually would suit them.
