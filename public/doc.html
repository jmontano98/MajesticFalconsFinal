Type in the 3 or 4 digit class code for the INST class you are looking for and select your class when it appears.
The next screen will show all of the most recent professors who have taught the course. Once you select your
section, you will see a graph of the last 3 semesters' average GPA.

By comparing average GPA over the last 3 semesters, we can obtain a decent snapshot of how the COVID-19
pandemic has affected the quality of a class overall, and can provide a recommendation on whether or not a
person should sign up for that specific section.
