Write in the 3 or 4 digit class code for your INST class you are looking for and press the button that appears.
This will show all the most recent professors who have taught the course and graph their last 3 semesters GPA.

By comparing GPA over the last 3 semesters, we can get a decent snapshot of how the COVID-19 Pandemic has affected the quality of a class overall,
and can provide a recommendation on whether or not a person should sign up for that specific section.
